EPL Definitions
  • Cycle - grouping of 10 games played within the span of a few days
  • Super Pick - Points bet on a team above the normal 10 points. May be 20 or 40 depending on the week and whether a super pick was used in the prior week.
  • Bonus Points - Double the wager made
Rules
  1. Punditz must submit picks (along with number of points) for each game before each game begins.
  2. Punditz are expected to submit picks via provided website.
  3. Punditz may submit one (1) team as a Super Pick per cycle. This is not mandatory.
  4. Punditz will be penalized for picking the same team as a Super Pick within a three (3) cycle window. i.e. Picking LIV in cycle 2 means that one would not be able to pick LIV again as a Super Pick until cycle 5 without incurring a penalty. Penalty is as follows:
    • Minus thirty (-30) for a wager of 20 points.
    • Minus fifty (-50) for a wager of 40 points.
  5. Any changes to picks must be done prior to the game start time.
  6. Failure to submit picks or submitting picks without points will incur minus fifteen (-15) points per game missed.
  7. There is no excuse for not submitting picks as the schedule is available weeks in advance.
  8. Punditz are awarded the points wagered for a win and half the points wagered for a draw for each game.
  9. Punditz will lose the points wagered for each game lost. e.g. if you wager six (6) points in a team and the team loses, you lose six (6) points.
  10. Punditz will lose twenty (20), or forty (40) points if the Super Pick loses the game.
  11. Bonus points are awarded for games where the winning team has won by three (3) or more goals. e.g. if you wager eight (8) points on a team and they win by three (3) or more goals, you are awarded sixteen (16) points.
  12. Bonus points are also awarded for the Super Pick.
  13. All decisions made by the Admin are final.
  14. Punditz are expected to submit a list of your predicted order of finish of teams (1st to 20th position) for the season. These predictions cannot be changed after they are submitted.
